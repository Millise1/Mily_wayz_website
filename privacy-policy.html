1. Data Protection Compliance

Mily Wayz is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our official website (the "Site").
We adhere to the relevant data protection laws and standards, including the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) issued by the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA). We also strive to meet the core principles of applicable international data protection standards where possible, ensuring fairness, lawfulness, and transparency in data processing.


2. Information We Collect

We limit the data we collect to what is necessary for the stated purposes. The types of data collected are:
  • A. Personal Data (Voluntarily Provided):
    • Email Address: Collected solely when you voluntarily use the newsletter sign-up form provided on the Site.
    • Basis for Collection: Consent (when you actively submit your email).
  • B. Usage Data (Automatically Collected):
    • We collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site through standard analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics).
    • Types of Data: Your computer's Internet Protocol address (IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.
    • Basis for Collection: Legitimate interest (to understand site performance and improve user experience).
3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following explicit purposes:

Data Type Purpose of Use
Email Address To send you updates, news, and promotional material about Mily Wayz, including new music releases, tour dates, and exclusive content.
Usage Data To analyze website traffic patterns, monitor Site usage, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve the overall user experience and Site performance.



4. Sharing and Disclosure of Data

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is shared only under the following limited circumstances:
  • A. Third-Party Service Providers (Data Processors):
    • Newsletter Management: We use a third-party service, Bandsintown (Bandsintown, LLC), to manage our mailing list, distribute newsletters, and track fan club interactions.
    • Data Transfer: When you sign up for the newsletter on our Site, your email address is immediately transferred to and held by Bandsintown, which acts as our data processor for this specific function.
    • Compliance: We have contracts with our service providers requiring them to protect your information in accordance with this Policy and the law.
    • Bandsintown Policy: We strongly encourage you to review Bandsintown's Privacy Policy for more details on how they handle your data, security measures, and international data transfers, as they are the direct custodian of your email address once submitted.
  • B. Other Third-Party Links:
    • The Site includes links to other external third parties (e.g., Spotify, Amazon, Vendblue). These parties operate under their own separate privacy policies. We have no control over the data they collect, and your interactions with them are governed solely by their respective terms.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under the NDPR and other applicable laws, you have specific rights regarding your personal data. These rights include:
  • Right to Access: The right to request copies of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: The right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure (Deletion): The right to request the deletion of your personal data.
Procedure for Exercise of Rights (Pertaining to Email Address):

Since your email address is held by Bandsintown on our behalf, you may:
  1. Directly manage your subscription using the "unsubscribe" or "manage preferences" link provided at the bottom of every newsletter email you receive. This is the fastest way to stop receiving communications and request data deletion from Bandsintown.
  2. Submit a formal request to Mily Wayz using the contact details in Section 8. We will then communicate your request to Bandsintown as our data processor to ensure your data is rectified or deleted in compliance with the NDPR.
