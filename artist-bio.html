Artist Bio
Mily Wayz is a passionate singer-songwriter
driven by his love for music. With a desire
to make his mark, he's dedicated to expressing
himself through his art. His stage name, "Mily Wayz,"
embodies his independence and determination to
pursue his dreams without validation.
Follow Mily Wayz for soulful melodies
and authentic storytelling.

Genre
Afro Pop

Style
His music style reflects his passion, individuality,
and emotional depth, blending African rhythms with
modern pop sensibilities.

This captures the essence of Mily Wayz's
artistry and music style!
