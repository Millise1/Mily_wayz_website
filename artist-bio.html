Mily Wayz

Mily Wayz is a Nigerian Afrobeats and Afro Pop artist and songwriter. Recognized for his independent spirit and authentic storytelling, his music blends traditional African rhythms with modern pop sensibilities. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, Mily Wayz is establishing a global presence through soulful melodies and a unique, self-determined artistic vision.

Musical Genre & Style

Primary Genre: Afrobeats / Afro Pop.
Style: Emotive storytelling paired with high-energy percussion and modern synth arrangements.

Digital Presence

Mily Wayz is active across all major streaming platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube. He engages with a global fanbase via X (formerly Twitter), Instagram, and TikTok.
