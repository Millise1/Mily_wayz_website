Artist Bio

Mily Wayz is a passionate singer-songwriter driven by his love for music. With a desire to make his mark, he's dedicated to expressing himself through his art. His stage name, "Mily Wayz," embodies his independence and determination to pursue his dreams without validation. Follow Mily Wayz for soulful melodies and authentic storytelling.
